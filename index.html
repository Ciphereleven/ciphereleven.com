Projects start from ₹50,000 (~$600 USD), priced per project — not per month. You pay once, we build and deploy, and you own it permanently with no recurring charges. Detailed quote provided after a free discovery call.

Your automation runs on your own servers — AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, DigitalOcean, or on-premise. Your data never flows through our systems. Complete sovereignty, GDPR, HIPAA, and IT Act compliant.
